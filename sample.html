The Strategy

Built on the StoryBrand Framework

StoryBrand is a proven messaging framework used by thousands of businesses worldwide. It positions your client as the hero of the story and you as the trusted guide who helps them succeed. Here's how it shapes every section of your new homepage.

1

Name the Problem

Your visitor arrives stressed about tax, drowning in paperwork, and worried about HMRC. The site immediately tells them: "We understand. You're not alone."

2

Position the Guide

Emma is presented as the empathetic expert — FCCA qualified, 20+ years' experience, Xero Silver Partner. Real testimonials and trust badges reinforce authority.

3

Give Them a Plan

A clear 3-step plan ("Book a Chat → Get a Quote → Relax") removes uncertainty. One repeating CTA makes the next step obvious: "Book Your Free Consultation."
